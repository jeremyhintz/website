Engineer

I started out college as a Mechanical Engineering major. While Mechanical Engineering didn't turn out to be my passion, the skills that it taught me have been invaluable in the early stages of my career as a software engineer. My ME courses were mathematically and analytically rigorous, and they emphasized the inevitablity of trade-offs. Those skills are directly transferable to programming, and I believe that the different perspective gives me a slight advantage over some of my peers.
